Throughout my internship at **Amazon Web Services Vietnam LLC** from **April 17, 2026** to **July 17, 2026**, I had the opportunity to participate in a professional work environment and gain exposure to enterprise-standard content deployment workflows. Classroom learning was directly connected to real-world practice through clearly defined tasks with weekly milestones and measurable outcomes.

During the internship, I went through 12 weeks of work divided into distinct phases. **The early phase (Weeks 1–6)** focused on building AWS fundamentals across topics including: VPC network architecture and Site-to-Site VPN, automated backup with AWS Backup and Amazon S3, EC2 server operations, multi-account access management with SSO and Cognito, and database systems including RDS/Aurora and DynamoDB. **The middle phase (Weeks 7–9)** focused on designing and refining the AI Assistant system architecture for the capstone project, while also participating in internal tech learning activities. **The final phase (Weeks 10–12)** involved hands-on deployment: deploying the Backend to AWS Lambda and connecting DynamoDB, deploying the Frontend to S3 and CloudFront, configuring Route 53, followed by End-to-End integration testing and completing the internship acceptance documentation. Through this process, I significantly improved my skills in **Serverless architecture design**, **AWS system deployment**, **technical documentation writing**, and **troubleshooting and incident analysis in production environments**.

In terms of work attitude, I always strived to complete tasks well, comply with workplace policies, and actively communicate with colleagues to improve work efficiency.
